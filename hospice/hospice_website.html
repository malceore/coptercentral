Swim a Mile for Hospice

What is Swim a Mile?

The first Saturday of August Hospice hosts the Swim a Mile event at Postwood Park Beach in Hannawa Falls, NY. This event is a non-competitive, open swim to raise money for Hospice and Palliative Care of St. Lawrence Valley. Speed is not the important thing. What is important is sharing a unique athletic experience on Hannawa Pond and raising money in the process.

Donate

Announcements: Thank You!

We welcomed 72 swimmers, 33 of them new to the event this year. Together, we raised nearly $24,000 to support Hospice services. Thanks to everyone who swam, volunteered and donated to this very important cause. We hope to see you in August of 2016!!!
